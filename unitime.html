Unitime : Day Journal Tool

Unitime is a prototype for a day journal tool that brings together an interest in the quantified self movement, productivity, and self-improvement. Its objective is to record how the day is spent focusing on developing specific areas of your life. The design and interactions all attribute to the zen and meditative nature of a journal.
