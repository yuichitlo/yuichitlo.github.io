True BU Campaign Website

True BU is one of two slates running for Student Government office in the current elections. Together with graphic designer Calvin Chin and the campaign's communications staff, we designed a website that portrays the simple and open approach this slate offers to the BU community. We decided to let the content and platform speak for themselves. Since the site is so minimalistic, it required some careful attention to design and development to make sure that it will give users a seamless and unobtrusive experience.
