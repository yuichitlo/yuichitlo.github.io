Celebrate the Mini Wins in Life!

"Celebrate the Mini Wins in Life!" is a personal mantra that I keep to remain thankful and optimistic despite the demands of everyday. This poster is a party in itself with vibrant colors, bold typography, and enough confetti to last all night long. Everything was hand cut and drawn with love and care. It was an extremely time consuming process, however it was great fun as well to return to my papercrafting roots and experiment with options organically throughout the design iterations.
