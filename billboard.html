2010 Retrospective: Billboard Hot 100

As an avid music listener, I love to follow the Billboard charts each week to observe what is the music of the times. Scraping data from the past five years for analysis, I attempted to mine the data and identify trends in the charts, song attributes, and lyrics for a poster presentation. To present the data in a more digestible manner, I designed a micro-site and series of visualizations to share the information with a wider audience.
