東京 : Tokyo Anthology

Curating and designing a 32-page anthology about the city of Tokyo paralleled the stressful yet serene environment one can find in the famed metropolis. The objective of this anthology was to establish a typographic system to anchor the varied content of the publication and as a whole, reflect the spirit of the city in a visual manner.

High contrast and boldly colored photos are balanced against the blocks of type and ample white space to pace the reader through the all the major aspects of architecture, cuisine, transport, and lifestyle.
