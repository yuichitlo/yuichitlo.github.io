Archer: Type Specimen Posters

Archer, designed by Tobias Frere-Jones in 2002, has been a commercially successful font featured most notably on Martha Stewart Living magazine and also in countless other media usages.

My objective in this series of type specimen posters is to exhibit the versatility of Archer in its ability to evoke a sense of polish and quirk, regardless of its setting as display, paragraph, and graphical type.
