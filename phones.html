How many phones?

Today's wasteful consumer culture and the never ending pace for better things has been reflected most clearly in the shift of cell phone life spans since their inception. Through animated illustrations paced according to year and underscored by the evolving nature of interpersonal communication and variety, my friend Thu and I hope to remind people to think about what we have now accepted as the norm.
