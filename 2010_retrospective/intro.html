2010s in Review:

Billboard Hot 100

The Billboard Hot 100 has long been a metric to gauge the way music is consumed. Its storied history began in 1958 and since then, the Hot 100 charts have chronicled the success of the largest artists of our time, tracked the spontaneous trends and ebb and flow of our cultural preferences, and adapted to the constantly changing relationship we have with music.

Let’s take a retrospective into the evolution of music in the 2010’s.
