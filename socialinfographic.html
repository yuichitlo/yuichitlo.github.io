"I Never Make Time for People" Infographic

Since I am constantly in workaholic mode, I tend to prioritize relationships and time for friends as the last thing on my mind. I intend on changing this though, so I embarked on a month-long study of my socializing patterns.

This infographic organizes the most interesting parts of my data and conclusions formed after the experiment. It seems I do make time for people after all!
